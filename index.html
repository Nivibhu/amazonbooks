Writing from a rare intersection of analysis and emotion

Nivedita Pandey is an economist and author whose work spans two seemingly different worlds. Trained at Banaras Hindu University, one of India's most prestigious institutions, she brings academic discipline and intellectual precision to everything she writes.

Her political writing reflects years of studying India's constitutional framework, electoral history, and democratic evolution. Her fiction explores the quieter revolutions that happen between two people who were never supposed to meet.

Currently preparing for the BPSC examination, she believes understanding institutions and understanding people are not such different things. Both require patience, empathy, and the willingness to look past the surface.
